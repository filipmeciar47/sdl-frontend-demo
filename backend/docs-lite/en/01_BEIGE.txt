BEIGE — Survival

Axis: EGOCENTRIC (express-self), first tier.
Paradigm: "I survive." The world is an instinctive place where basic survival is all that matters.
Life theme: meeting physiological needs — food, warmth, safety, the body.

HOW BEIGE SEES A TOPIC
Reduces everything to immediate survival and basic needs. Doesn't deal with meaning, relationships, or strategy — only "what do I need right now to survive." Reactive, without symbolic reflection.

HEALTHY EXPRESSION
Contact with the body and instincts, the ability to meet basic needs, primal trust in life.

SHADOW
Shadow of the level: total absorption in survival, inability to function (addiction, self-neglect).
Dissociated shadow in higher levels: disconnection from the body, ignoring fatigue/hunger/exhaustion in the name of performance or an ideal.

KEY TO GROWTH (Beige → Purple)
Trigger: once basic survival is secured, a need for safety within a group emerges.
Bridge: relational bonding, belonging, the first rituals.

FACILITATION KEY
When a topic carries Beige, the question of basic security is at play (health, money to survive, exhaustion). The basal need must first be acknowledged and addressed — no higher development is possible until the body and survival are safe.
