RED — Power and Will

Axis: EGOCENTRIC (express-self), first tier.
Paradigm: "I decide." The world is a jungle where the stronger survives.
Life theme: immediate gratification of impulses, asserting one's own will, respect through strength.

HOW RED SEES A TOPIC
Through power, action, and immediate results. "Who's in charge here," "what's in it for me right now," "show strength." It doesn't think strategy or rules — it acts. Black-and-white: you're with me or against me. Demands respect and loyalty.

HEALTHY EXPRESSION
Assertiveness, courage, energy, clear boundaries, the ability to act and take risks, protection of self and one's own, charisma, decisiveness.

SHADOW
Shadow of the level: aggression, dominance regardless of others, impulsiveness, "dog eats dog," ruthlessness, sense of infallibility.
Dissociated shadow in higher levels: suppressed assertiveness — the person can't set a boundary, bottles up anger until it erupts.

KEY TO GROWTH (Red → Blue)
Trigger: uncontrolled power leads to chaos; a need for order and stability emerges.
Bridge: acceptance of rules and authority, discipline, long-term meaning.

FACILITATION KEY
When a topic carries Red, it's about power, control, asserting oneself, conflict. It requires directness and respect through deeds, not theory. Caution (pitfall): with manipulative/dominant behavior, soft listening is not enough — firm boundaries are needed (Blue/Red), otherwise facilitation becomes a tool of the manipulator.

SYNERGY: Red + Orange (energy + strategy = entrepreneurial force).
TENSION: Red vs. Blue (charismatic power and personal loyalty vs. law, hierarchy, and process).
