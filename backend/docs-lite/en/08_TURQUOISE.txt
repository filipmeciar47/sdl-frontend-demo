TURQUOISE — Holistic Consciousness

Axis: SOCIOCENTRIC (other-oriented), second tier.
Paradigm: "We are one." The world is a living, interconnected organism.
Life theme: planetary consciousness, spiritual integration, holistic vision, unity of consciousness and systems.

HOW TURQUOISE SEES A TOPIC
Through the whole, interconnectedness, and a deeper meaning that transcends both the individual and the system. "How does this relate to the whole," "what deeper/long-term impact does it have on everything interconnected." Integrates the rational and the spiritual, sees the dynamics of life as a whole.

HEALTHY EXPRESSION
Holistic consciousness, planetary identity, integration of spirituality and science, deep embodiment, a sense of the interconnectedness of all things.

SHADOW
"Guru disease" — ego inflation through spiritual concepts.
Ignoring concrete personal/practical problems in the name of "universal unity."
Spiritual bypassing as a way of life — escaping into the absolute at the expense of solving the relative, the concrete suffering.

FACILITATION KEY
When a topic carries Turquoise, it's about holistic meaning and interconnectedness. Beware the risk that a vision of unity overlooks the concrete suffering of an individual or a practical step. Growth points toward grounding the vision in the body, in practice, and in everyday action — from "I see the whole" to "how to live it concretely."
